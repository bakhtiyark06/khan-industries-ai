## What Is the Khan Industries Operating System (KOS)?

The **Khan Industries Operating System (KOS)** is the collection of documents inside this `docs/` folder. Together, they define how the company operates across every discipline — company strategy, brand, products, services, website, engineering, AI agents, sales, roadmap, and product requirements.

The KOS is not software. It is the **shared operating manual** for the company. It captures the decisions, standards, and principles that should guide every action taken inside Khan Industries, so that work remains consistent regardless of who — or what — is doing it.

Think of the KOS as the **constitution of the company**:

- It defines *who we are* (company and brand).
- It defines *what we build* (products, services, AI agents).
- It defines *how we build* (engineering and website standards).
- It defines *how we grow* (sales, roadmap, and product requirements).

Every contributor — human or AI — is expected to align their work with the KOS.

---

## Why the KOS Exists

Khan Industries is built to scale over many years. As the team, product surface, and number of contributors grow, knowledge cannot live only in one person's head or in scattered conversations.

The KOS exists to:

1. **Create a single source of truth.** One place that explains how the company works.
2. **Preserve intent.** Capture *why* decisions were made, not just *what* was decided.
3. **Enable consistency.** Ensure every page, product, and AI agent feels like it came from the same company.
4. **Onboard quickly.** Let any new engineer, contributor, or AI assistant become productive fast.
5. **Protect quality.** Encode the standards that keep the work premium, professional, and trustworthy.
6. **Support long-term maintainability.** Make the company understandable and extendable for years to come.

Without the KOS, quality and direction drift. With it, the company stays aligned as it grows.

## How to Use This Documentation

This documentation is meant to be **read, referenced, and respected** — not skimmed once and forgotten.

**For everyone:**

- Read this index (`README.md`) first.
- Read the documents in the recommended [reading order](#documentation-reading-order) before contributing.
- Treat these documents as the authoritative standard. When in doubt, the KOS wins.

**For engineers and AI assistants:**

- Before building any feature, confirm your approach aligns with `05_ENGINEERING.md` and the relevant product or website document.
- Before building or modifying an AI agent, follow `06_AI_AGENTS.md`.
- Always honor the **Honesty Policy** described in `00_COMPANY.md`: never invent clients, testimonials, revenue, awards, certifications, or company history.

**When the work and the docs disagree:**

- If reality has changed, **update the documentation** as part of your work — do not let the docs go stale.
- If you are unsure, raise the question rather than guessing. The KOS should be improved deliberately, not silently bypassed.

**Document numbering:**

- Files are numbered `00`–`09` to communicate a deliberate reading sequence, from foundational identity to execution detail. Lower numbers are more foundational; higher numbers are more operational and project-specific.

## Documentation Library

Each document in the KOS has a single, clear responsibility. The descriptions below explain what belongs in each file.

### `00_COMPANY.md` — Company Foundation

The foundation of everything. Defines the company's **mission, vision, values, company philosophy, and long-term direction**. Establishes who Khan Industries is, what it stands for, the principles that guide decision-making, and the legacy it aims to build. Every other document inherits its spirit from here.

### `01_BRAND.md` — Brand & Identity

Defines the company's **brand identity, design language, voice and tone, color system, logo philosophy, and customer experience principles**. Ensures every touchpoint — website, product, agent, proposal, and communication — looks and feels unmistakably like Khan Industries.

### `02_PRODUCTS.md` — Products

Defines the company's **current products, future products, product strategy, and product lifecycle**. Explains how products are conceived, prioritized, built, evolved, and eventually retired, and how each product supports the company's mission.

### `03_SERVICES.md` — Services

Defines the company's **current services, future services, pricing philosophy, and customer offerings**. Explains what Khan Industries delivers as services, how those services are scoped and positioned, and the principles behind how they are priced.

### `04_WEBSITE.md` — Website

Defines the **website architecture, navigation, user experience, component strategy, SEO, and future expansion**. Serves as the blueprint for the official website — both how it is structured today and how it should grow to support the marketplace, dashboards, portals, and other future surfaces.

### `05_ENGINEERING.md` — Engineering

Defines the company's **coding standards, architecture principles, Git workflow, folder structure, documentation standards, testing, deployment, and engineering practices**. This is the technical rulebook every engineer and AI assistant must follow to keep the codebase clean, scalable, and production-ready.

### `06_AI_AGENTS.md` — AI Agents

Defines the **standards every AI agent built by Khan Industries must follow**, including architecture, quality expectations, deployment philosophy, documentation requirements, and maintenance. Ensures every agent the company ships is reliable, well-documented, and worthy of the Khan Industries name.

### `07_SALES.md` — Sales & Client Relationships

Defines the **consultation process, sales philosophy, lead qualification, proposals, onboarding, CRM process, and long-term customer relationships**. Explains how Khan Industries turns interest into trust, and trust into long-term partnerships, in a way that is honest and premium.

### `08_ROADMAP.md` — Roadmap

Defines the **company roadmap covering short-term, medium-term, and long-term goals**. Provides shared visibility into what is being built now, what is planned next, and where the company is heading over time.

### `09_PRD.md` — Product Requirements

Holds the **Product Requirements Documents (PRDs) for active projects and future software initiatives**. Translates strategy into concrete, buildable specifications — defining goals, scope, requirements, and success criteria for each initiative.

## Documentation Principles

The KOS follows the same standards as the company it describes:

- **Truthful.** Documentation reflects reality. It never invents clients, testimonials, revenue, awards, certifications, or company history.
- **Clear.** Written to be understood quickly by humans and AI assistants alike.
- **Organized.** Each document has one responsibility and a predictable structure.
- **Maintainable.** Easy to update as the company evolves, with intent preserved alongside decisions.
- **Premium.** The quality of the documentation reflects the quality of the company.

---

## Maintaining This Documentation

The KOS is a living system. Keeping it accurate is part of doing the work well.

- When a decision, standard, or direction changes, **update the relevant document** in the same effort.
- Keep each document focused on its single responsibility; do not duplicate content across files.
- When adding a new document, give it the next sequential number, update this index, and add it to the [reading order](#documentation-reading-order).
- Prefer improving an existing document over creating a redundant one.

A well-maintained KOS keeps Khan Industries aligned, fast, and trustworthy as it grows.
